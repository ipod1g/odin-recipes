Beef and Mushroom Lasagna

A random image of lasagna I found from the net with delicious looking colours.

Ingredients
  • 90% lean ground beef 450g
  • Small onion 1pc
  • Portobello mushrooms 2pcs
  • Part-skim mozzarella cheese 3cups
  • Olive oil 1 Tbsp
  • Secret tomato sauce 4 cups
  • And extra love 1 tsp
Steps
  1. Finely dice the onion and mushrooms
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat
  3. Add onions and mushrooms to soften
  4. Add beef and tomato sauce to combine
  5. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees celsius
  6. Place the pan in oven to bake for 1 hour after sprinkling mozzarella cheese
